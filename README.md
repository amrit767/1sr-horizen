number guessing game made in darkmode theme. 

Taken inspiration from my nintendo.

it generate number between 1 to 100.

it gives feedback regarding thta is it higher than the hidden number or lower than the hidden number.

it has a attempt counter whuch tells how many attempts have been commited by the user till now and on which attempt his / her guess was correct and on which attempt was the guess higher / lower than the guess.

it is saved in the guess history.

we can press the buttun guess or press enter to guess.

after winning autommaticly a new round emerges.
